A Day in the Life of a Reforestation Volunteer in Brazil



This summer, our own Ashley Junger traveled to the spectacularly mountainous Reserva Ecológica de Guapiaçu (REGUA) to take part in the expedition Wildlife and Reforestation in Brazil . She surveyed small mammals in an old-growth forest with Dr. Manoel Muanis and other Earthwatch volunteers. This is the first step toward monitoring how mammal populations respond to reforestation efforts throughout the reserve. Now, Ashley is sharing her experience and what it’s like to trek the forest as a volunteer on the expedition Wildlife and Reforestation in Brazil.
